Manage insolvency and redundancy claims

Employees and directors can use this service to:

  • claim money if your employer owes you a redundancy payment or other money like wages, holiday and commission
  • claim money if your employer owes you a payment relating to unserved notice period
  • amend a claim for payment

Directors claiming money must also use this service to provide additional information.

Non-employee's who are owed payments from employers, like contractors, are known as creditors. Creditors can use this service to:

  • register as a creditor to find contact details of case handlers if you are owed under £1,000
  • submit a proof of debt if you are owed over £1,000

Insolvency practitioners can use this service to:

  • set up a redundancy case
  • provide employee and employer information relating to insolvency and redundancy claims
